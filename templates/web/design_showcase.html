03. Reading Layouts

Standard Reading (.ei-reading)

The reading class restricts the maximum width to 65 characters to ensure optimal readability for long-form content. It automatically handles paragraph spacing and font families.

It also includes special styling for blockquotes:

"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."

This layout is perfect for standard blog posts and articles.

Case Reading (.ei-case-reading)

The case reading class is slightly wider (72 characters) and is intended for case studies or deeper technical documentation.

Subheading Level 2

Notice how the headings within the case reading layout use the display font but maintain sentence case instead of full uppercase, providing a more formal structure without being overly aggressive.

  • It supports unstyled lists.
  • With custom indentation.
  • And proper spacing.
